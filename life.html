Life Lessons:
  • before you buy, research: warranty, return policy, interest rate, early termination fee, alternatives.
  • choosing contentment over desire will bring you peace.
  • cost more does not mean worth more.
  • don't buy things from salespeople. you'll be sorry.
  • give portable code only the options they need.
  • make life about the people not the money.
  • tech is shinier when it's not yours.
  • wait before you buy.
  • when the product is free, you are the product.
